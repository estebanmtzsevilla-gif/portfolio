Centeo

2020. Fintech, UI design

As part of Centeo’s product team, I supported the translation of their defined strategy into polished, high-fidelity interfaces. My role focused on shaping layouts, components, visual consistency and enhanced usability to ensure the product vision was accurately reflected in the user experience.

Outcomes

Improvedtrust and accessibility aligning Centeo’s positioning with the everyday needs of Mexican business owners
Streamlined onboarding flow improved clarity and reduced drop-offs
Clearer product communication for SMEs reducing barriers to understanding and adoption

Context

Centeo is a fintech company providing fast, flexible financing for SMEs in Mexico. Backed by global investors and advanced analytics capabilities, their mission was clear: democratize access to world-class financial services and drive business growth.

However, their initial brand expression leaned heavily on financial jargon and global positioning, which risked alienating local SMEs that needed clarity and trust above all.

Challenge

How might we translate Centeo’s sophisticated vision into a digital experience and communication style that was approachable, culturally relevant, and easy to understand for Mexican business owners—without losing credibility with partners and investors?

Solution

My role focused on UI design execution, ensuring that the company’s strategic intent was reflected in clear, consistent, and user-friendly interfaces:

Simplified Communication
Reframed complex financial language into straightforward messages, with layouts that highlighted benefits over technical detail.

Interface Design
Translated wireframes and flows into polished high-fidelity UI screens, applying visual hierarchy to guide users effortlessly through applications and service details.

Consistency & Trust
Extended and applied Centeo’s visual identity into a cohesive UI library, ensuring every interaction felt modern, professional, and trustworthy.

Local Relevance
Introduced UI elements and visual cues tailored to resonate with a Mexican SME audience, balancing accessibility with fintech credibility.
