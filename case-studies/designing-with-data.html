Scaling design

2020-2025. Financial services/Fintech, UX leadership, Product design

For over five years, I've helped transform our team into a strategic driver of the company's digital evolution.
The design team evolved from being a service provider of isolated outputs into a strategic driver of experience enhancement. Design became involved earlier in the process, influencing product direction, improving operations, and enabling better business decisions through evidence, insight and systems thinking.

Our mission was to establish a data-driven, customer-centered practice that created alignment across stakeholders and delivered measurable business outcomes across the entire ecosystem (web, app, physical touchpoints and fintech products).

My leadership focused on building capabilities instead of dependencies.
We scaled research literacy. We matured design ops. We bridged silos between tech, marketing and product. And most importantly: we made design matter.

More importantly, the mindset shifted: Data became a shared language between design, product, and business.

Context

In our organization, product and design decisions were often driven by personal opinions, hierarchy, and internal assumptions, resulting in solutions misaligned with real user needs.

Challenge

How might we foster a culture where design decisions are grounded in evidence and user insights, minimizing bias and delivering more relevant, high-impact experiences?

Solution

We launched Designing with Data, an initiative to embed a data-driven mindset into our design practice, tackling it from three fronts:

Analytics Infrastructure Integrated analytics tools into our digital products (starting with the app) to track key interactions, establish baselines, and measure the impact of improvements.
Early Evaluation Protocols Formalized pre-release testing stages, including proofs of concept, prototypes, and usability tests with real users ensuring decisions are guided by validated insights, not isolated perceptions.
Strategic User Research
Conducted a generative study of the SME segment to redefine our value proposition across the website and digital ecosystem.
Planned a deep-dive study on cardholders to identify specific use cases, paving the way for hyper-personalized experiences.
