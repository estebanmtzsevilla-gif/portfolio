Crypto wallet

2019. Fintech, Product design

Redesigned core currency screens and wallet interactions to improve usability and consistency across the platform. Focused on simplifying flows, updating UI components, and ensuring a trustworthy, cohesive experience for crypto users.

Outcomes

Consistency across currencies strengthened brand presence and user trust.
Improved clarity of key interactions and data visualization.
Enhanced usability for both novice and experienced users.

Context

Tauros' currency screens had limited usability, with outdated UI patterns and no clear hierarchy of information. Users struggled to interpret key data, reducing the effectiveness of the trading experience.

Challenge

How might we improve the currency screens to provide clearer information, better usability, and consistency across different digital assets?

Solution

  • Conducted a usability analysis to identify friction points in current flows.
  • Proposed a redesign introducing new components such as clear price display, transaction buttons, and contextual actions.
  • Applied consistent color coding and visual hierarchy across multiple currencies (BTC, BCH, Dash, Stellar, Litecoin).
  • Designed flexible states (including empty states) to handle different usage scenarios.
